HTU




HTU was established to provide modern technical and applied education, focusing on vital fields demanded by the job market. Inspired by Crown Prince Hussein’s vision, HTU empowers Jordanian youth to build a brighter future.

Located in the heart of King Hussein Business Park, Al Hussein Technical University (HTU), established by the Crown Prince Foundation, is a pioneering institution addressing the gap between technical graduates and employment opportunities. HTU offers a unique model of technical education, combining academic excellence with practical experience to meet evolving industry demands. By equipping students with cutting-edge skills and knowledge, HTU prepares them to excel in their fields, shaping the future of technical education and empowering Jordanian youth.

Mission

Empowering graduates with advanced skills and adaptability, preparing them to excel in today’s ever-changing technical and professional landscape.


Strategic Goal

Acadia HTU aims to be a globally competitive technical university, bridging education and industry to produce skilled, innovative graduates ready for modern STEM careers.


Vision

To become a global leader in technical education, fostering innovation and empowering individuals to make a positive impact on society."
